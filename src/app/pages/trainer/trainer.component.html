Praveen Chirayath is a distinguished corporate trainer with over 20 years of experience in language instruction and personal development. As the founder of The Next Level, an educational institute based in Thrissur, Kerala, he has been instrumental in enhancing English language proficiency among learners. His expertise encompasses language skills, interview techniques, and personality grooming. In June 2023, Praveen achieved a significant milestone by setting a record in the Indian Book of Records for teaching English grammar, underscoring his dedication to education.
